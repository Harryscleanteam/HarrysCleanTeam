Our Gym Cleaning Speciality

We Know Gyms From
the Inside Out

Harry's background is not just cleaning — it's fitness. Before founding Harry's Clean Team, Harry spent years working in and managing gym and fitness facilities. He knows what a gym looks like at its best, what it looks like when cleaning is neglected, and exactly what it takes to keep a fitness environment truly hygienic.

That hands-on experience as a gym operator means Harry's Clean Team doesn't just clean gym surfaces — we understand member expectations, peak-hour traffic patterns, high-risk hygiene zones, and the equipment care that keeps a gym running well and its members coming back.

When you hire Harry's Clean Team for your gym, you're hiring people who have stood where your members stand — and who hold your facility to the same standard they'd want for themselves.

🦠

Hygiene Where It Matters Most

Gyms are high-touch, high-sweat environments. We prioritise the surfaces that carry the highest germ load — equipment handles, weight collars, cable attachments, and mat surfaces — using commercial-grade disinfectants safe for rubber and metal.

🚿

Change Room & Shower Standards

Change rooms are where members form their strongest impressions. We deep-clean showers, benches, lockers, and floor drains to eliminate odour, mould, and bacteria — delivering the standard members expect from a quality facility.

🏃

Scheduled Around Your Members

We know gym traffic. Early morning, lunchtime peak, after-work rush — we schedule cleans to fit your opening hours and member flow, not the other way around. After-hours and pre-opening cleans available.

📋

Custom Gym Checklist

Every facility is different. Free weight rooms, group fitness studios, pools, saunas, crèches — we build a bespoke checklist for your gym so nothing is missed and nothing is over-cleaned unnecessarily.
