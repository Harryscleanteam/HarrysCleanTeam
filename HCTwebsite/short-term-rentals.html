What's Included

Our Standard STR Checklist

🛏️ Bedrooms & Living Areas

  • Strip and remake all beds with fresh linen
  • Dust all surfaces, shelves, and skirting boards
  • Vacuum carpets and mop hard floors
  • Clean mirrors and glass surfaces streak-free
  • Empty bins and replace bin liners
  • Arrange cushions, throws, and décor presentation

🍳 Kitchen

  • Clean and sanitise all benchtops
  • Clean stovetop, oven exterior, and rangehood
  • Wipe appliances inside and out
  • Clean and empty fridge of any guest leftovers
  • Wash, dry, and replace all dishes
  • Restock consumables to agreed levels

🚿 Bathrooms & Laundry

  • Scrub and sanitise toilets, showers, and baths
  • Clean vanity, taps, and mirrors
  • Replace towels with fresh, folded sets
  • Restock soap, shampoo, and toiletries
  • Mop floors and clean laundry area
  • Remove hair and guest residue from all drains

🌿 Outdoor & Entry Areas

  • Sweep verandahs, decks, and entry paths
  • Wipe down outdoor furniture
  • Clean BBQ grill and surrounding area
  • Remove leaf litter and visible debris
  • Check and tidy mudroom or boot entry
  • Final walkthrough quality check before departure
